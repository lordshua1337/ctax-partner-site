Your Roadmap

Partner Onboarding Checklist.

From application to first commission check. Here's exactly what happens when you join.

Submit Your Application
Fill out the partner application form. Takes about 5 minutes. We'll review and respond within 1 business day.
Intro Call with Partner Manager
A 15-minute call to discuss your practice, choose the right tier, and set revenue expectations. We'll answer all your questions.
Sign Partner Agreement
Review and sign your partnership agreement. Covers revenue share rates, terms, and compliance responsibilities. Fully digital.
Get Portal Access
Receive login credentials for the Partner Portal. Track referrals, view commissions, and access all marketing materials in one place.
Complete Training (30 min)
Short training on how to identify clients with tax debt, make the referral conversation, and use our AI tools (Script Builder, Revenue Lab, Ad Maker).
Submit Your First Referral
Log in to the portal, fill out the referral form (2 minutes), and we take it from there. Your client gets a call within 24 hours.
Earn Your First Commission
Once your client signs up for resolution services, your revenue share is calculated and added to your next payout. Most partners see their first check within 30-45 days.
